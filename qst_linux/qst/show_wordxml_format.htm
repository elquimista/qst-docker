Multiple choice - single answer questions (15 answers max.), Multiple choice - many answers, Short Answer and Paragraph questions may be imported.

** Text questions will import the Explanations and Memory to the questions.

Open your word .doc (or .txt document) and make sure you put QST (with a space after it) as the first word at the beginning of each question.

Each answer should have )@ at the begining of the line with each answer.

Each correct answer should have ** as the first letters of the answer.

The characters )@ and ** and QST should not be used in any questions or answers as they are special characters in the import format.

Once you have done the above, save your word doc as xml (and .txt as .txt) and import it.

An example of Multiple Choice:

QST What is the bird below?

   

)@ duck
)@ owl
)@
)@ ** pheasant
